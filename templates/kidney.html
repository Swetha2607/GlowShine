Kidney-Friendly Foods

  • Best: Cabbage, cauliflower, garlic, onions, bell peppers
  • Fruits: Apple, berries, grapes, pineapple
  • Protein: Egg whites, fish, chicken (small portions)

Stone Prevention

  • Drink 2.5-3L water + lemon daily
  • Reduce salt (excess sodium = calcium in urine)
  • Limit oxalate: spinach, beets, chocolate, excess tea

Diabetes Prevention

  • Carbs <50-55% of daily calories
  • Choose low GI foods (GI < 55)
  • Fiber 25-30g/day slows sugar absorption
  • No sugary drinks — #1 cause of spikes
  • Walk 15 min after meals — reduces glucose by 30%

Daily Checklist

  • 2+ eggs or 50g+ protein daily
  • 1 cup dal/lentils (iron + zinc)
  • Handful of almonds/walnuts
  • 1 amla or citrus fruit
  • Green leafy vegetable
  • 2.5L+ water
Hair killers: Excess sugar, crash dieting, dehydration, high sodium, stress

5 Rules

  • Calorie deficit: Eat 300-500 kcal less than TDEE
  • Protein first: 1g per kg body weight
  • Fiber up: 25-30g/day (oats, dal, veggies)
  • Move daily: 7000-10000 steps + 20 min activity
  • Sleep 7-8h: Poor sleep increases hunger hormones
